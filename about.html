About

Welcome to the most preeminent destination for equisite movie criticicm. You will witness nothing short of insightful and engaging reviews of the latest works in the entertainment industry to hit both the big screen and small screen. Our team of two, Anbumozhy T.S. and Nandika Suresh, and their passion for movies, years of essential reviews, and ability to connect with readers serve as the site's foundation.

We bring to our users not only current reviews of films from major studios, but one of the most comprehensive coverage of reviews from independent and documentary film makers, as well as reviews of leading television or cable series and shows from various streaming platforms.

We're committed to creating a vibrant and welcoming community of movie lovers, and we invite you to join us.

So sit back, grab your popcorn and join in the conversation!
